Recreational tennis for all skill levels

Founded in 2003, Tennis at Cal is a recreational tennis club on campus available to tennis
players of all skill levels. There are no tryouts and anyone can join. So whether you're a seasoned
USTA player or a total newbie, feel free to join us on the courts!





Weekly Practice

We hold practices 3 times a week, 2 hours a session. Feel free to drop in anytime to play some fun games and meet new people!


Tournaments

We host a singles, doubles, and team tournament every semester, all included in member dues. Follow our Facebook page for more info about signups, and click here for a list of past winners.


Socials

Leave your racket at home for our social events such as group bowling trips, Wimbledon viewing parties, and other socials. Connect with friends outside the tennis courts!


Membership

Membership is $10/semester. Click this link to fill out our online membership form, and flag down any of our officers at practice to pay either by Venmo or cash. The first week of practices each semester is open to everyone, so feel free to come check us out!

Interested in joining?

Follow our Facebook page or get added to the mailing list for the most up-to-date information.
